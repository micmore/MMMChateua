Experience a taste of the ocean with our Maine lobster served with Arroz Negro (squid ink rice), a truly indulgent seafood dish. Our succulent lobster is carefully prepared by our expert chefs to ensure every bite is tender and flavorful, and is perfectly complemented by the rich, savory flavor of our squid ink rice.

Our Story

Welcome to our restaurant, a family-owned fine dining establishment that has been serving the Chicago community since 1949. Located on the North Side, our restaurant has a rich history of quality and excellence that has been passed down through generations of our family.

We believe that dining is not just about the food, but also about the experience. That's why we've created a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can relax and savor every moment. We invite you to join us and experience the magic of our restaurant.
